Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how WhereIsIt ("the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

WhereIsIt is designed to work locally on your device. The app may collect and store the following data only on your device:

  • Photos you take within the app
  • Text notes and labels you create
  • Location or place descriptions you manually enter

2. How We Use Your Information

All data is stored locally on your device and is used solely to provide the core functionality of the app — helping you remember where you left your belongings. We do not send your data to any server or third party.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties. Your notes and photos never leave your device.

4. Camera Permission

The app requests access to your device's camera to allow you to take photos of object locations. Photos are stored locally and are never uploaded.

5. Storage Permission

The app requests storage access to save and retrieve photos and notes on your device.

6. Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you choose to delete it within the app or uninstall the app. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.

7. Children's Privacy

WhereIsIt does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not require account creation or any personal identification.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
